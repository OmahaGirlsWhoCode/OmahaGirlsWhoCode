Applying

How old does my daughter have to be?

Girls Who Code is for girls in 6-12th grade. Some clubs only have a class for 6-8th graders or 9-12th graders. Please read the clubâ€™s application when applying.

How much does it cost for my girl to participate?

Nothing! With the help of volunteers and sponsors, the club is free for girls. However, we are not able to offer transportation to or from Girls Who Code classes or events.

Does my girl need a laptop?

Not during class, but it is also useful for your daughter to have access to a computer outside of class. The only requirements are that the computer have the most recent version of the Chrome browser installed and access to the internet. But apply either way. We do not want to stop any girl from participating because she does not have access to a computer. Please let the club know when applying if your daughter needs access to a computer, during or outside of class.
